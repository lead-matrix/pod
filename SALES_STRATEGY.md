# ThreadDrop — $100K/21-Day Launch Playbook 🚀

To hit a target of **$100,000 USD in 21 days**, you need to generate approximately **$4,761/day**. Assuming an Average Order Value (AOV) of **$65** (e.g., 2 shirts or 1 premium hoodie), your target is **73 orders per day**. 

Here is the operational and sales strategy playbook to achieve this scale using the ThreadDrop platform.

---

## 📈 Operational Math & Conversion Funnel
To consistently hit 73 orders/day at a standard streetwear conversion rate of **3%**, your traffic funnel looks like this:
*   **Daily Unique Visitors:** 2,430 targeted visitors.
*   **Add to Bag Rate (Target):** 8% to 10% (driven by the visitor counter and scarcity alerts).
*   **Checkout Completion:** 30% to 35% of cart additions (minimized by Stripe Checkout's 1-click Apple Pay/Google Pay integration).

---

## ⚡ Phase 1: Operational Readiness (Days 1–3)
Before launching the traffic sources, configure these components to prevent fulfillment bottlenecks:

### 1. Stripe Checkout Settings
*   **Enable 1-Click Payments:** In Stripe Dashboard > Settings > Payment Methods, turn on **Link**, **Apple Pay**, and **Google Pay**. This cuts checkout times down to under 5 seconds.
*   **Create Coupons:** Create your discount codes (e.g., `LAUNCH10`, `VIP20`) in Stripe > Billing > Coupons > Promo Codes. These are now supported directly in your ThreadDrop checkout screen.

### 2. Printful Wallet Funding
*   **Pre-fund your Wallet:** Printful fulfills automatically only if there is money in your Printful Wallet or a valid card linked. Since orders are placed automatically, ensure your Printful billing profile is funded to avoid order suspension.

### 3. Vercel & Supabase Environment Setup
Ensure your live environment variables match the keys in [.env.demo](file:///f:/GIT/POD/.env.demo):
*   `VITE_SUPABASE_URL` and `VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY` on the Vercel frontend.
*   `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`, `STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET`, and `PRINTFUL_API_TOKEN` on the Supabase Edge Functions.

## 📣 Phase 2: Traffic & Viral Playbook (Days 4–21)
Traditional paid ads (Facebook/Google) take too long to optimize for a 21-day timeline. Instead, leverage organic short-form video and high-impact micro-influencers.

### 1. The Micro-Influencer Seed Campaign (AOV Booster)
*   **The Strategy:** Identify 50-100 creators on TikTok/Instagram with 5k to 50k followers in your niche (streetwear, skater culture, anime, fitness, etc.).
*   **The Offer:** Send them free clothes. Use the ThreadDrop Admin Panel to create an order manually for them (setting price to $0).
*   **The Loop:** Give each influencer a custom 10% off code (created in Stripe). Offer them 10% commission on all sales generated by their code.

### 2. Scarcity & Drop Model (CRO Optimizer)
*   **The Strategy:** Do not list 100 designs at once. Launch with **3-5 exclusive designs** under a "DROP 001" theme.
*   **Urgency Window:** Announce that the drop is only available for 72 hours or until sold out. 
*   **Visual cues:** The low-stock badge ("Only a few items left in size L") and the viewer counter ("🔥 17 people are viewing this right now") will drive rapid checkouts.

### 3. Short-Form Video Engine
*   **The Strategy:** Post 3x daily on TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
*   **Content Angle:** Do not post plain product images. Post behind-the-scenes content of you designing the products, matching outfits, styling guidelines, and packaging review orders.
*   **The CTA:** Direct viewers to the Link in Bio. Use a discount code specifically for organic social media traffic (e.g., `TIKTOK15`).

---

## 🛠️ Automated Retargeting & Retention
*   **Abandoned Cart Loop:** If a user logs in but leaves items in their cart, our secure DB sync tracks it. Send them a recovery discount after 4 hours.
*   **Post-Purchase Viral Hook:** On the success page, encourage customers to share their purchase on Instagram/TikTok tagging your brand to receive a code for their next order.
